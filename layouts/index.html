About

Hi, I am Ian Teda and this my story so far:

I enjoy a good adventure, and cannot help but wonder what is around the next corner. My most exciting adventure at the moment is raising my two young boys – Austin and Dillan – with my beautiful wife Laura.

I have been fortunate to work as a Civil Engineer on some amazing infrastructure projects within NSW and ACT Australia. Some highlights from that work, that I am proud to say I was involved in include: balanced cantilever segmental bridges; dry well intake tower and new RCC dam; bulk earthworks; the manufacture of pre-tensioned bridge elements such as pile caps, Super T beams, planks and Reinforced Concrete Box Culverts; the Sydney Light Rail design.

My hard work and determination has come with the satisfaction of seeing large infrastructure projects being born, projects that I am proud to tell my boys I have worked on.
